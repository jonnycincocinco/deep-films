What we do

Placement

In today’s hyper-targeted media world, it you don’t have a good idea exactly who you want to reach, and it can be brutally precise nowadays, you should sit down and have a good, long think. On the other hand, when you know you are, say, reaching liberal Labradoodle owners with a household income of $120,000+, it’s go time. Not only can we make targeted films for these folks, we can rock their furry little world.
