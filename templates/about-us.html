About Us

Rob Bagot

In the 7th grade a jingle writer from Foote Cone Belding visited Rob’s junior high at lunchtime. He was casually dressed. Told funny stories. Showed his reel. And kids laughed milk out of their nostrils. Rob had found his calling.

After graduating from Berkeley, Rob landed his first job at BBDO writing ads for Apple computer. From here, he joined Goodby, Berlin & Silverstein where he won most every national and international advertising award. Over the last 20 years, rob has served at the creative director- or chief creative director-level at such agencies as Hill-Hollday, McCann-Erickson and Ogilvy & Mather on accounts that include IBM, Microsoft, Cisco, Coca-Cola, Toyota and McDonalds.

In the mid-2000s Rob’s infatuation with the Canon 5D camera led him to make his own films and eventually directing commercials. He directed – and in many cases wrote – most of the work on this site.

Rob is incredibly stoked to live 40 seconds from work with his family in Sausalito, California.

Anastacia Maggioncalda

Anastacia’s love of content creation began when she was a photography and cultural anthropology student at Sarah Lawrence College in New York. After graduating and completing her internship at Magnum Photos, she began he career in New York working as a producer and studio manager alongside fashion photographers Troy Word, Ruedi Hoffman and others. She then made the transition to commercial production when she took on the role of Head of Production at Shelter Films, followed by a move to Crossroads Films and then had an extensive stint as a freelance producer in New York and Paris.

In time, she headed West to work as the Senior Executive Producer at Radium in both the LA and SF offices and Spy Post before acting in the same role at L.inc Design. She moved to the agency side of things as the landscape began to evolve and production, post and the agency world began to collide. Antastacia has worked with brands such as BMW, Gatorade, Target, Lexus, Coke, Virgin America, Oakley, Apple, Visa, Aria, Union Bank, Dignity Health, AAA and Sprint and have run visual effects production on music videos for artists like N'SYNC, Ricky Martin, Linkin Park, Sheryl Crow, Missy Elliot, Kanye West and on feature films including Spiderman, Hell Boy, Castaway, Sin City and Iron Man.

Her all time favorite project was managing the visual effects on one of the first web film series made: BMW Films starring Clive Owen. The series was directed by award-winning directors as Ang Lee, Guy Ritchie, John Woo, Wong Kar Wai and the late John Frankenheimer, and all Executive Produced by David Fincher.

Anastacia lives in an Eichler home in Terra Linda with her husband and her awesome and highly entertaining children.
